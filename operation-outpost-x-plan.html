JEDI TRAINING MISSIONS

8 Missions • Led by Master Matthane Sol • For Youngling Syba Taan
MISSION 1

AWAKENING THE FORCE

Day 1 • Evening • 15 min • Fire Pit
MASTER MATTHANE SAYS:

"The Force is all around us, Syba Taan. It flows through every rock, every flame, every gust of wind. A Jedi does not see the Force — a Jedi feels it. Close your eyes. Tell me what the Force is showing you."

THE ACTIVITY

Blindfold Sybastian with a bandana near the fire pit. He must sit still, breathe, and identify 5 sounds around him: the wind, fire crackling, footsteps (Matthew walks around him), Megan clapping, a rock tapping. Each correct answer is a point. Master explains how Jedi use the Force to sense what they cannot see.

PROPS NEEDED
  • Bandana (blindfold)
  • Small rocks to tap together
REWARD
⭐ Padawan Bead #1 — "Force Awareness"
MISSION 2

THE WAY OF THE LIGHTSABER

Day 2 • 9:00 AM • 25 min • Open area at OutpostX
MASTER MATTHANE SAYS:

"A lightsaber is not just a weapon, Youngling. It is an extension of you — of your will, your focus, your courage. Today, I will teach you the three sacred forms. Watch closely."

THE ACTIVITY

Matthew teaches Sybastian 3 lightsaber forms using their own lightsabers:

  • The Shield — hold saber horizontal in front (defense)
  • The Strike — overhead downward swing (attack)
  • The Sweep — side-to-side sweep at knee height

Practice each 5 times slowly, then 3 times at speed. End with a slow-motion "duel" between Master and Youngling (for Megan's camera!).

PROPS NEEDED
  • Both lightsabers (Sybastian's own)
  • Pool noodle targets (optional, for striking practice)
REWARD
⭐ Padawan Bead #2 — "Form I: Shii-Cho"
MISSION 3

THE SAND CRUISER PATROL

Day 2 • 10:00 AM • 30 min • OutpostX property
MASTER MATTHANE SAYS:

"The Council has received reports of unusual activity on the far side of this outpost. We must patrol the perimeter. Stay sharp, Padawan — report anything unusual you see."

THE ACTIVITY

During the Sand Cruiser ride, Sybastian is the "Scout." He must spot and call out 5 interesting things he sees (unique rock formations, plants, structures, animals, shadows). Matthew confirms each sighting and adds Jedi lore. Use the OutpostX audio tour as the "intelligence briefing."

PROPS NEEDED
  • Sand Cruiser (OutpostX amenity)
  • Small notepad for "mission log" (optional)
REWARD
⭐ Padawan Bead #3 — "Outer Rim Scout"
MISSION 4

THE FORCE BALANCE

Day 2 • 11:00 AM • 20 min • Trampoline area
MASTER MATTHANE SAYS:

"A Jedi must command their body as well as their mind. Balance. Control. Grace. The Force flows through a body that is centered. Show me your balance, Youngling."

THE ACTIVITY

On the in-ground trampolines at OutpostX, play "Jedi Simon Says." Matthew calls out moves and Sybastian copies:

  • Force Jump — high jump with arms up
  • Jedi Freeze — land and hold completely still for 5 seconds
  • Spin Attack — gentle spin jump
  • Grogu Bounce — tiny fast bounces with hands in fists (Baby Yoda ears!)

End with both of them doing a synchronized "Force Jump" together.

PROPS NEEDED
  • In-ground trampolines (OutpostX amenity)
REWARD
⭐ Padawan Bead #4 — "Force Balance"
MISSION 5

CRAFTING A JEDI ARTIFACT

Day 2 • 2:00 PM • 45 min • Pottery studio
MASTER MATTHANE SAYS:

"Every Jedi creates something with their own hands — a relic imbued with the Force. It becomes part of you. Today, you will craft your artifact from the clay of this desert world. Focus your energy into it."

THE ACTIVITY

Use OutpostX's pottery wheels to create a small bowl, cup, or "Kyber crystal holder." Matthew and Sybastian each make one. No need for perfection — it's about the focus and the memory. Megan can make one too or photograph the process.

PROPS NEEDED
  • Pottery wheels (OutpostX amenity — included)
REWARD
⭐ Padawan Bead #5 — "Jedi Artisan" + the pottery piece itself
MISSION 6

TRACKING THE KYBER CRYSTAL

Day 3 • 9:30 AM • 90 min • Snow Canyon State Park
MASTER MATTHANE SAYS:

"Deep in the canyons of this world, the Force has hidden a Kyber Crystal — the heart of every lightsaber. The crystal calls to those who are worthy. Listen, Padawan. Follow the signs. The crystal will reveal itself to you."

THE ACTIVITY

Hike Jenny's Canyon (0.3 mi, very easy, slot canyon — incredible scenery) and the Petrified Sand Dunes (1.2 mi, gentle). Megan pre-hides a crystal/geode at a scenic spot along the trail. Matthew gives Sybastian 3 "clues" at landmarks:

  • Clue 1: "The crystal is past the narrow passage" (slot canyon)
  • Clue 2: "Look where the ancient dunes turned to stone" (petrified dunes)
  • Clue 3: "It hides where a Jedi would stop to rest" (a scenic rock shelf)

When Sybastian finds the crystal, Matthew kneels and says: "The crystal chose you, Syba Taan."

PROPS NEEDED
  • Crystal or geode (buy beforehand — wrap in cloth)
  • 3 clue cards (print or handwrite on aged-looking paper)
  • Water bottles, sunscreen, snacks for the hike
REWARD
⭐ Padawan Bead #6 — "Crystal Guardian" + THE KYBER CRYSTAL
MISSION 7

THE FINAL TRIALS

Day 3 • 3:00 PM • 30 min • OutpostX grounds
MASTER MATTHANE SAYS:

"You have trained well, Youngling. But before you can be knighted, you must pass the three Final Trials. These will test your body, your mind, and your heart. Are you ready?"

THE ACTIVITY

Three mini-challenges, each 5–10 minutes:

Trial of Balance: Walk a straight line (draw in sand or use a rope) heel-to-toe for 10 steps without wobbling. Three attempts allowed.

Trial of Focus: Stack 5 rocks into a cairn (rock tower) without it falling. Master and Youngling do this together, in silence.

Trial of Courage: Stand tall, look Master Matthane in the eye, and recite the Youngling Jedi Code from memory:

"I am calm.
I am kind.
I am brave.
I am one with the Force."

Teach Sybastian this code earlier in the day so he can practice!

PROPS NEEDED
  • Rope or stick (for the balance line)
  • Flat-ish rocks (find on property)
REWARD
⭐ Padawan Bead #7 — "Trials Complete — Ready for Knighting"
MISSION 8

THE KNIGHTING CEREMONY

Day 3 • 7:30 PM • 15 min • Under the stars
MASTER MATTHANE SAYS:

"Syba Taan. You came to this outpost a Youngling. You have trained in the ways of the Force. You have wielded a lightsaber. You have tracked a Kyber Crystal across the desert. You have passed the Final Trials. You are ready."

THE CEREMONY

Outside under the stars at OutpostX. Fire pit glowing nearby. Megan filming and photographing.

  4. Matthew says: "By the will of the Force and the authority of the Jedi Council, I knight thee Jedi Knight Syba Taan. Rise, young Jedi."
  5. Sybastian stands. They bow to each other.
  6. Family hug. Sparkling cider toast.
PROPS NEEDED
  • Lightsabers (lit up for the ceremony)
  • Junior Jedi Knight Certificate (print beforehand)
  • All 7 Padawan beads displayed
  • Sparkling cider + cups for the family toast
REWARD
🌟 JEDI KNIGHT SYBA TAAN — Certificate + all 7 beads + the Kyber Crystal

🌯 Mos Eisley Cantina Wraps

Day 1 — Dinner
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 lb chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 6 large flour tortillas
  • 1 container hummus
  • Mixed greens
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • Shredded cheese
  • Olive oil, cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper
DIRECTIONS
  1. Season chicken with cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper
  2. Cook in skillet with olive oil, 6–7 min per side until done
  3. Slice chicken into strips
  4. Spread hummus on tortilla, layer greens, chicken, veggies, cheese
  5. Roll up, slice in half
BLUE MILK (side drink)
  • 2 cups milk + 1 tbsp vanilla + 2 tbsp honey + 3 drops blue food coloring
  • Blend with ice for milkshake version. Serve in fun cups.
FOR SYBASTIAN
  • Fruit cup on the side (grapes, berries, banana slices)

🔥 Campfire Feast

Day 2 — Dinner (fire pit)
KEBAB INGREDIENTS
  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 bell peppers (red + yellow), chunked
  • 2 zucchini, thick slices
  • 1 red onion, chunked
  • Wooden skewers (soak 30 min first)
  • Marinade: olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, salt, pepper
FOIL PACKET POTATOES
  • 4 medium potatoes, diced small
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika
  • Optional: diced onion, butter
DIRECTIONS
  1. Marinate chicken cubes 30+ min (can do this while resting)
  2. Toss diced potatoes with oil + seasonings, wrap in heavy-duty foil packets
  3. Place foil packets on fire pit coals — cook 20–25 min
  4. Thread alternating chicken and veggies onto skewers
  5. Grill kebabs over fire 10–12 min, turning every few minutes
S'MORES DESSERT
  • Graham crackers + marshmallows + chocolate bars
  • Roast marshmallows over fire, assemble, enjoy
DRINK: "JOGAN FRUIT PUNCH"
  • Mixed berry juice served in camp cups

🥣 Rebel Morning Rations

Day 3 — Breakfast (prep the night before!)
OVERNIGHT OATS
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Toppings: berries + banana for Sybastian, granola for adults
DIRECTIONS
  1. Night before: Mix oats, milk, chia seeds, honey, vanilla in jars/containers
  2. Refrigerate overnight
  3. Morning: top with fruit and eat cold (or warm up)

🥩 Jedi Council Feast

Day 3 — Dinner — THE BIG ONE
GRILLED STEAK
  • 2 ribeye or NY strip steaks (1–1.5 lbs total)
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • Butter + fresh rosemary (optional)
DIRECTIONS
  1. Let steaks rest at room temp 30 min
  2. Season generously: salt, pepper, garlic powder
  3. Grill or cast iron over fire — 4–5 min per side for medium
  4. Rest 5 min, top with butter and rosemary
  5. Slice thin for Sybastian's portion
ROASTED VEGGIES (foil packet)
  • Asparagus or green beans + cherry tomatoes
  • Toss with olive oil, salt, pepper
  • Wrap in foil, cook over fire 15–20 min
GARLIC BREAD
  • Baguette or pre-made frozen garlic bread
  • Warm over the fire or grill in foil
DESSERT: LIGHTSABER PRETZELS
  • Pretzel rods + green/blue candy melts
  • Melt candy, dip pretzel rods ← instant mini lightsabers!
  • (Prep at home, bring ready-made)
CELEBRATION TOAST
  • Sparkling apple cider in camp cups — saved for the Knighting!
